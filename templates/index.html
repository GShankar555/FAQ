How to join MLSC ? (Microsoft Student Learn Chapter)

There will be two rounds.First round is quiz that will be conducted on basics of Microsoft tools and second round is interview where student will be evaluated by their professional skills and projects.
Note* you need to be qualified in round 1 in order to participate in round 2
